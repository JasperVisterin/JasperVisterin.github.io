Distributed Delay Controller Optimization

The optimization framework minimizes the spectral abscissa: $$\min_{\mathbf{p}} \max \{\operatorname{Re}(\lambda)\}$$ where \( \lambda \) are the characteristic roots of the plant $P$ depending on controller parameters \( \mathbf{p} \). In this case, the Distributed Delay Controller is studied.
