Distributed Delay Controller Optimization

The optimization framework minimizes the spectral abscissa: $$\min_{\mathbf{p}} \max \{\operatorname{Re}(\lambda)\}$$ where \( \lambda \) are the characteristic roots of the plant $P$ depending on controller parameters \( \mathbf{p} \). In this case, the Distributed Delay Controller is studied and $K_1, K_2$ and $c$ make up the parameters $\mathbf{p}$. $$ C \leftrightarrow u(t)=K_{1} y(t) + K_{2} \int_{-\tau}^{0} g(\theta) y(t+\theta) d \theta $$ $$ g(\theta)=\sum_{k=0}^K c_k T_k\left(2 \frac{\theta}{\tau}+1\right) $$
