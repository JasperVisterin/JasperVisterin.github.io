Distributed Delay Controller Optimization

The optimization framework minimizes the spectral abscissa: $$\min_{\mathbf{p}} \max \{\operatorname{Re}(\lambda_i)\}$$ where \( \lambda_i \) are the rightmost eigenvalues depending on controller parameters \( \mathbf{p} \).

The video above shows the evolution of the spectrum as parameters are tuned. Note how the rightmost eigenvalue moves leftward.
